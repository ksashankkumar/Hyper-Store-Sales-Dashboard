# Hyper-Store-Sales-Dashboard
The Hyper Store Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze and visualize sales performance across different regions, product categories, customer segments, and months. The dashboard provides valuable business insights by tracking revenue, profit, units sold, and product performance.
PROJECT OBJECTIVES :
* Analyze overall sales performance and profitability.
* Track monthly revenue and net profit trends.
* Compare units sold across different months.
* Identify the best-selling and worst-selling products.
* Analyze revenue by customer segment and product category.
* Enable interactive filtering by region and year.
  DASHBOARD FEATURES :
* Total Revenue
* Net Profit
* Units Sold
* Total Unit Cost
* Revenue & Profit Trend by Month
* Revenue & Units Sold Analysis
* Top 5 Best-Selling Products
* Top 5 Worst-Selling Products
* Revenue by Customer Segment
* Revenue by Product Category
* Interactive Region Filter (North America & South America)
* Year Filter for Dynamic Analysis
KEY INSIGHTS :
* Identified the highest and lowest-performing products based on sales.
* Monitored monthly revenue and profit trends.
* Compared revenue across different customer segments.
Evaluated sales performance by product category.
Analyzed units sold and overall profitability to support business decisions.
